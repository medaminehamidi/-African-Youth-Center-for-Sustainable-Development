African Youth Center for Sustainable Development

Creating opportunities for youth

A Moroccan center that promotes integrated approaches to the implementation of the sustainable development goals, through leadership and capacity-strengthening of youth and the creation of development project ideas based on global cooperation.

  • It is a non-governmental organization that aims to contribute to the empowerment of youth and women in Morocco, with the aim of promoting civil, economic, social and cultural rights through comprehensive community projects.
  • The activities of the association do not have a political character. It exercises its activities freely; its positions are based on human values, tolerance, acceptance and openness to the outside world
  • African Youth Center for SDG’S is a gathering of youth full of passion, motivation and eager to learn and share under the umbrella of the Agenda 2063
  • Being the leading center for training youth for leadership roles, in the light of future challenges to contribute to achieving the sustainable development goals plan in light of future challenges.
  • Creating opportunities for youth that contribute to their inclusion in the development plan SDG’.
  • Strengthening the knowledge capacity of young people and creating opportunities for them.
  • Conducting studies that contribute to innovation and sustainable development.
  • Focus on contributing to support the right to education, innovation and environmental preservation.
  • Being the leading center at the African and Arab levels in the field of promoting the values of tolerance and coexistence among peoples.
  • A development-inspired youth center that works together to put ambitions into action.

A social values incubator
The African Youth Center for Development is a Moroccan institution that aims to contribute to the empowerment of youth and women in Morocco with the aim of promoting civil, economic, social and cultural rights through comprehensive community projects. Our organization is the result of a gathering of young people with diverse and rich careers. Our goal is unique. Comprehensive and sustainable impact to achieve this, the organization works through the following actions:
Research
By preparing and publishing research, as well as organizing activities and conferences to encourage youth participation, contribute to change and integrate them into development affairs.
Coaching
Contribute to building youth capabilities and strengthening skills through training programs under the supervision of experts in various fields.
Promoting
civic and social rights recognized by the various international treaties (Universal Declaration of Human Rights, the Covenants of Rights, Agenda 2030, Agenda 2063).
Socio-economic actions
For the benefit of youth and the marginalized, especially in rural areas, and the inclusion of women through accompaniment and technical support in partnership with local development agencies.

Each person has a unique contribution to make. Our task is to recognize, encourage and value each person's contribution - including our own! We believe in commitment. Everyone is ready to be part of our community.

A bunch of enthusiastic and creative minds & we are a youth-led organization that aims to:

  • Strengthening the foundations of dialogue and coexistence among young people through the creation of initiatives.
  • Encouraging studies and research that support African development at various levels.
  • Building youth capacities through a group of soft skills workshops.
  • Contributing to sustainable local development through comprehensive projects and creating partnerships and cooperation.
  • Awareness of the need to follow a participatory approach in achieving the SDG’S goals plan and work on developing competencies and skills to develop various resources.
